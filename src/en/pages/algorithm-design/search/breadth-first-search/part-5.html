5. Paths — Formal Definition

A path of length k is a sequence:

v0, v1, …, vk

such that:

(vi−1, vi) ∈ E

for all i = 1, …, k.

The length of the path is k.

Shortest path problem: min k, subject to the existence of such a sequence.
