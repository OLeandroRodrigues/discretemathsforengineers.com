The Core Idea of BFS

Intuition

BFS explores the graph in layers.

Key concepts

  • Level sets L0, L1, L2, …
  • Wavefront expansion
  • FIFO discipline (queue)
Engineering insight: BFS is a shortest-path algorithm for unweighted graphs.
